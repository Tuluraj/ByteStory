Top Refer-and-Earn Programs in India

Unlock hidden income streams by leveraging the most lucrative referral opportunities.

Paytm Money

PayTM Money offers attractive rewards through its referral program, with ₹300-400 for each friend who opens a demat account using your unique code. The platform provides lifetime revenue sharing up to 50% from brokerage commissions on referred accounts. Share your referral link via WhatsApp to help friends access stocks, mutual funds, and gold investments. Referrals must complete their first transaction within 7 days to unlock rewards.

Key Features

  • ₹300-400 per referral
  • Lifetime revenue sharing up to 50%
  • Refer via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media
  • First transaction within 7 days to unlock rewards

Earning Potential

With an active user base of over 7 million, the earning potential from the Paytm Money referral program can be significant for those who effectively leverage their network.

PhonePe

PhonePe offers ₹100-₹1000 through their referral rewards system. Download the app, enter the code 'AMANBJZP' during sign-up, and complete your first UPI transaction to receive cashback via scratch cards. Share your referral link with friends via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media. Both you and your friend earn rewards when they make their first transaction of ₹100 or more.

Key Features

  • ₹100-₹1000 referral reward
  • Cashback via scratch cards
  • Refer via WhatsApp, SMS, or social media
  • First transaction of ₹100+ to unlock rewards

Earning Potential

With PhonePe's user base of over 300 million, the referral program offers significant income-generating opportunities for those who can effectively leverage their network of friends and family.

Amazon Pay

Earn up to ₹10,000 through Amazon Pay's referral program. For each friend who completes their first transaction, you'll receive ₹75 in your Amazon Pay balance. Share your referral code on social media platforms, and target frequent online shoppers by highlighting Amazon Pay's secure payment features.

Key Features

  • Earn up to ₹10,000 in referral rewards
  • ₹75 per friend who completes first transaction
  • Promote on social media and to online shoppers
  • Leverage Amazon Pay's trusted brand and features

Earning Potential

With Amazon's massive user base and the growing popularity of digital payments, the Amazon Pay referral program offers significant income potential for those who can effectively reach and convert new users.
